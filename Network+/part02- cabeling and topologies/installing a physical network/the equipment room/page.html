what is in the equipment room?

1- MDF: main distribution frame is the main frame where the networking happens
2- IDF: intermediate distribution frame is another frame that connects to the main frame. for example multiple floors company would have one MDF and multiple IDF on each floor or so

the equipment rack is devided into what known as Us simply is units. the U is equal to 1.3/4 inch

3- demarc: that seperates the equipment of the phone company and your equipment. it provides internet connection to the MDF

4- demarc extension: this is when the building has multiple offices and everyone should have different setup.
